Overview

More consumers are choosing high-deductible healthcare plans, which also increases their financial responsibility. Instead of wasting time sending statements and following up on outstanding balances, your organization can automate the entire process.


In this webinar, Inovalon’s Senior Product Manager, Jeremy Swanson, shares the details of the rise in patient financial responsibility. Swanson also discusses the challenges this presents to healthcare organizations and how streamlining billing can increase revenue while enhancing the patient experience.


By watching the webinar, you will discover how you can:

Simplify billing

Automate recurring payment plans to help reduce manual follow-ups and improve staff productivity and morale

Capture more revenue

Create branded statements that optimize patient understanding and expedite payments for faster collection

Enhance patient experiences

Provide convenient, flexible payment options with a 24/7 online payment portal and easy-to-read branded statements
